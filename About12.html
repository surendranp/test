About Us

Hope Research Foundation is a Non Governmental Organisation (NGO) working in the field of addiction rehabilitation. The staff members have all had very personal experience of the perils of addiction and have found a way out through Divine Grace and professional help. Hope Research Foundation offers a wide range of services to those suffering from addiction, including counseling, group therapy, and detoxification. We also offer support to families who are affected by addiction. Our goal is to provide sufferers with the tools they need to lead healthy, productive lives.

Mission

Our mission at Hope Research Foundation is to provide the best drug and rehabilitation services to individuals seeking recovery from substance abuse since 20018 . We are committed to offering a safe, compassionate, and supportive environment where individuals can heal, regain control of their lives, and reintegrate successfully into society.

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as the leading drug and rehabilitation center, not only locally but globally, for our exceptional care, innovative treatments, and successful outcomes. We aspire to continuously improve our services and expand our reach to help more individuals overcome addiction and achieve lasting recovery.

Values

We prioritize compassion, excellence, a holistic approach, integrity, collaboration, and continuous improvement in all aspects of our drug and rehabilitation services.
